Our Story

Our story began with a simple observation: finding reliable construction workers is still a major challenge for many people in Sri Lanka.

Customers struggle with inconsistent pricing, lack of transparency, and difficulty locating trusted technicians. At the same time, talented construction workers do not receive steady job opportunities and often depend on offline methods or middlemen.

After speaking with constructors and several technicians in Jaffna, we clearly understood the real pain on both sides. That was the moment we realized — there was a huge gap in the construction industry.

We asked ourselves a simple question:
“If food delivery and ride booking have digital platforms, why not construction services?”

This question sparked the creation of Site Smart — a dedicated construction service marketplace designed to bring fairness, transparency, and convenience to both customers and technicians across Sri Lanka.

Today, we proudly connect thousands of homes with verified professionals — from Mannar to Jaffna.
